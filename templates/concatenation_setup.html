Description

What is it?

Merging pre-recorded bits of audio to generate audio for words and sentences.

How does it work?

Speech concatenation works by recording every phoneme and then stitching the recordings back together to create new sequences of those phonemes.

Please note, this setup has been prepared exclusively with British English in mind. This is because a completely different dictionary would needed to map words to phonemes for each dialect. Results may therefore vary for any dialect other than British English.
